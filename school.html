    School career

    My journey in computer science began in high school at ITIS C. Zuccante in Mestre, Venice, where I acquired a solid foundation in programming languages and data structures. This early experience ignited my passion for technology and prepared me for more advanced academic challenges. I then pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Data Science at Ca’ Foscari University of Venice, where I deepened my knowledge of data analysis, algorithms, and mathematical modeling. Through numerous courses in probability, statistics, and data analysis, I developed a rigorous analytical mindset that continues to inform my approach to technical problems. Building on this background, I completed a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity—also at Ca’ Foscari University—with a focus on system and software security, network defense, and web application protection. This program equipped me with the specialized skills needed to understand and address complex security challenges in modern computing environments. My educational path reflects a clear and intentional progression: from foundational programming, through data-driven methodologies, to advanced cybersecurity expertise. Each step has strengthened my technical abilities, fueled my curiosity, and reinforced my commitment to contributing meaningfully to the field of computer science.
