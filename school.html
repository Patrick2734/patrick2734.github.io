    School career

    My journey in learning computer science-related subjects begins in high school. I attended the ITIS C. Zuccante in Mestre, Venice, where I gained important knowledge on programming languages and their structures. Subsequently, I pursued a three-year bachelor's degree in Data Science at Ca' Foscari University in Venice, where I deepened my knowledge on data analysis and structures. I took numerous mathematics courses on probability, statistics, and data analysis. Now, I am currently enrolled in a Master's degree in Cybersecurity at Ca' Foscari University with the aim of deepening my knowledge of security, from system and software security to network and web security.

    My educational path in computer science subjects starts in high school. I attended the ITIS C. Zuccante in Mestre, a location in the city of Venice. At this institution, I found myself immersed in a stimulating environment where I could acquire fundamental knowledge on programming languages and their structures. This early phase of my educational journey laid down a solid technical foundation, preparing me for more complex challenges that I would encounter later on.

    After having consolidated my basic skills, I decided to continue with a three-year bachelor's degree in Data Science at Ca' Foscari University, also located in Venice. This course of study allowed me to significantly deepen my knowledge in the field of data analysis and data structures. I had the opportunity to take numerous mathematics courses centered around probability, statistics, and data analysis, disciplines that are fundamental for effectively understanding and interpreting data.

    Currently, I am continuing my academic journey by enrolling in a Master's degree in Cybersecurity at Ca' Foscari University. This master's program is designed to provide me with specialized training in a field increasingly in demand, namely cybersecurity. The goal is to deepen my knowledge across all aspects of security, from system and software protection to network and web security. This course of study will enable me to tackle emerging challenges in the cybersecurity landscape, preparing me to make a significant contribution to the field.

    Therefore, my educational path is characterized by a logical and cohesive progression, starting from learning the fundamentals of programming to deepening advanced specializations in fields of high demand such as data science and cybersecurity. Each step of my educational journey has contributed to shaping my passion for computer science, fueling my curiosity, and my willingness to continue learning and growing in the field.
